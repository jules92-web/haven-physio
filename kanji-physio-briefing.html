Exercise (Durga Protocol) Detail
Paw flip exercise Tickle hair between back paw pads (fingers or soft brush) for 2 min. Wait for her to flip paw to correct position. Repeat 5× per paw.
Sling standing Towel or sling under belly, lift to natural standing. Use small treats. ~10 min per session.
Cycling exercise Lying on side: 10 cycles per leg. Later, repeat in standing position when ready.
Sling walk Very slow 5-min walk using sling. Surface must have good grip — sand or grip mat. Not smooth flooring.
Hydrotherapy Stand in chest-deep warm water under supervision only. Stop immediately if she pants heavily.
Laser therapy Spine and hind legs. Alternate days initially, then every 3 days.
No food before physio Small amount fine for energy if needed. Full meal causes bloating and lethargy — avoid.
